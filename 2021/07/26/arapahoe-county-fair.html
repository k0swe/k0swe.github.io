ARES Event: Arapahoe County Fair

Jul 26, 2021

For the past 4 days I was assisting Arapahoe County ARES with the Arapahoe County Fair. I’m not sure what I was expecting, but this event turned out to be very challenging and gratifying!

My role was to walk around the fair as part of a team of 2 or 3 and be extra eyes and ears for the sheriff’s office and the fair staff. During the course of each day, I ended up averaging about 10 miles walking. We assisted with about 10 lost child incidents where we searched for children or parents; 3 or 4 medicals where we called the event EMTs out into the fair to help people; and asked for sheriff support on a couple of unruly customers. Good times!
