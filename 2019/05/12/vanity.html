Vanity Callsign Came In

May 12, 2019

My amateur callsign is now K0SWE.

Many people use their initials, but combinations of K0 and my initials end up with rather unfortunate connotations. Instead, I chose something with my job title, “software engineer,” which at my workplace is abbreviated as SWE.
